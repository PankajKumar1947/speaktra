# 📘 PRODUCT & TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION DOCUMENT

## Project Name (Working)

**Speaktra**

## Product Vision

It is a **domain‑specific, speaking‑first English learning app for working professionals** that focuses on **real confidence at work**, not textbook English. Speaktra prioritizes daily speaking over passive learning.

---

## 1. TARGET USERS

### Primary Users

- Working professionals (Corporate, IT, Sales, Healthcare)
- Non‑native English speakers

### Core Pain Points

- Fear of speaking in meetings
- Limited professional vocabulary
- Hesitation & lack of fluency
- Existing apps feel childish or generic

---

## 2. CORE VALUE PROPOSITION

> Speak confident, professional English daily — customized for your job.

Key differentiators:

- Domain‑specific content
- Speaking‑first experience
- Daily structured practice
- Confidence‑based progress metrics
